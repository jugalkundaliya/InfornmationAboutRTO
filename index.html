Regional Transport Office (RTO)


The Regional Transport Office or Regional Transport Authority (RTO / RTA) is the organisation of the Indian government responsible for maintaining a database of drivers and a database of vehicles for various states of India. The RTO issues driving licences, organises collection of vehicle excise duty (also known as road tax and road fund licence) and sells personalised registrations. Along with this, the RTO is also responsible to inspect vehicle's insurance and clear the pollution test. Function of RTO To enforce the provisions of the various acts of motor vehicles, central motor vehicle rules and the State motor vehicle rules as laid down by the government from time to time. To ensure that co-ordinated development of road transport through management of permit. To charge and collect tax as per the provisions of the motor vehicle act.
